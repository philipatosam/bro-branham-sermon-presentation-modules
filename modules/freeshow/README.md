# FreeShow Module

**File:** `bro-branham-sermons.fsb`
**Format:** FreeShow Bible module (json-bible standard)
**Size:** ~95 MB

## ⬇️ Download

➜ **[Download bro-branham-sermons.fsb from Releases](https://github.com/philipatosam/bro-branham-sermon-presentation-modules/releases)**

## 📥 Import into FreeShow

1. Download `bro-branham-sermons.fsb` from the Releases page
2. Open FreeShow
3. Click the **Scriptures** drawer in the left panel
4. Click **+** → **Import Bible**
5. Browse to `bro-branham-sermons.fsb` and select it
6. The module will appear as **"Bro. William Branham — Messages"**

## 🔍 Searching

Once imported, you have two ways to find a sermon:

**Browse panel** — The center of the Scriptures drawer lists all 1,206 sermons in chronological order. Click any sermon to open it, then click a paragraph to add it to your presentation.

**Bottom text search** — Click the magnifying glass icon at the bottom of the Scriptures drawer to search by words across all 208,274 paragraphs. Type any phrase (e.g. *"faith is the substance"* or *"seventh seal"*) and it will return every matching paragraph across all sermons.

## 🗂️ How Sermons Are Structured

Each sermon is treated as a **book** with **one chapter** and each **paragraph as a verse** — exactly like a Bible reference.

| Element | Example |
|---|---|
| Book (sermon) | `47-0412 · Faith Is the Substance` |
| Chapter | Always Chapter 1 |
| Verse (paragraph) | Paragraph number (e.g. ¶44) |

## ⚠️ Long Paragraphs

`warnings.txt` in this folder lists 90 paragraphs that exceed 3,000 characters. These are included in the module but may display as unusably long slides. They are flagged for a future manual splitting pass.
